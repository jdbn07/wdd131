Guayaquil, Ecuador (EC)

This is one of the best curiosities of Ecuador for nature lovers. Ecuador has more birds per square kilometer than any other place in the world. With 1,632 different confirmed species and 48 other hypothetical species, there are plenty of feathers to beat. They also hold the world record for having the most hummingbird species, with an incredible 132 different types of birds calling this country home. Did you know that hummingbirds can only be found in the Americas? Also, hummingbirds are the smallest bird, with the highest metabolism in the world. In fact, their metabolism is so high that they have to enter a state of hibernation every night. This is called torpor and they do it to conserve energy.
