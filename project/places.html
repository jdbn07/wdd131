Museo Municipal

Address: Sucre Street between Chile Street and Pedro Carbo Street, downtown.

Activities: Exhibition of historical and cultural elements.

History: It is the oldest museum in the city, more than 100 years old. It has a permanent exhibition with the history of the city, a natural history room, more than 12,000 museum pieces and around 3,500 monthly visitors. Its exhibitions and rooms are constantly renovated, they also constantly offer workshops and cultural activities. It is open from Tuesday to Saturday from 9 am to 5:30 pm and they have a very educational podcast, if you want to know more about the culture of Guayaquil.

Address: Samborondon

Activities: Walk, photographs, viewpoints,family activities

History: There are 8 hectares of land divided into three zones: wildlife zone; the urban architecture zone; and the tradition area.
